# CineMatch

Your personal movie companion. Discover, track, and share your cinematic journey.

## Login and Register

![Login Page](ss2_login.png)

Users can sign in or create an account to access their personalized space. The login page gives a preview of what CineMatch offers before you're even in.

## Browse

![Browse Page](ss4_browse.png)

The main hub. Scroll through the full movie catalog with a featured pick at the top. Popular movies and personalized recommendations sit side by side on the same page.

## Search and Filters

![Search](ss6_search.png)

Live search pulls up movies as you type. The sidebar lets you narrow results by genre, release year, minimum rating, and streaming platform.

## Movie Details and Rating

![Movie Modal](ss7_movie_modal.png)

Click any movie to see the full details including cast, director, runtime, and a trailer link. From the same modal you can rate it out of 5 stars, add it to your watchlist, and write a review.

## Watchlist

![Watchlist](ss8_watchlist.png)

Everything saved to watch later in one place. Each entry shows the date it was added and links back to the movie details.

## Recommendations

![Recommendations](ss_recommendations.png)

Personalized picks based on your highest rated genres using rule-based logic. If you have no rating history yet, the system falls back to the most popular movies on CineMatch and prompts you to start rating.

## Trending

![Trending](ss9_trending.png)

The most active movies on the platform ranked by combined ratings and watchlist saves. Switch between 7, 30, and 90 day windows to see what's hot right now versus over time.

## CineBlend

![CineBlend](ss3_cineblend.png)

Enter a friend's username and CineBlend computes a compatibility score based on overlapping top genres. It then generates a list of movies neither of you has rated yet, starting with the top pick for your movie night.

## Community Awards

![Awards](ss10_awards.png)

Monthly highlights calculated automatically from real platform activity. Covers the most active reviewer, highest rated movie, most watchlisted film, and the genre of the month.

## My Dashboard

![Dashboard](ss11_dashboard.png)

A private stats page showing your total movies rated, average rating, reviews written, and watchlist count. Includes a genre breakdown and rating distribution chart. Only visible to you.

## User Profile

![Profile](ss5_profile.png)

Your public page showing genre preferences, favorite decades, rating stats, and recent activity. You can share it or keep the watchlist section private.

## Admin Panel

![Admin Panel](ss1_admin.png)

Admins search TMDB and add movies to the library with one click. The panel shows platform-wide stats and lets admins edit metadata or remove any movie from the catalog.

## Tech Stack

- Python, Flask
- MS SQL Server
- TMDB API
- HTML / CSS / JS
